Our average response time is 15-30 minutes, depending on your location and traffic conditions. We prioritize emergency situations and always aim to reach you as quickly as possible.

We offer a wide range of roadside assistance services including:
  • Emergency towing
  • Battery jump-start
  • Tire replacement
  • Fuel delivery
  • Lockout service

Once your service request is accepted, you can track the mechanic's location and estimated arrival time through our live tracking feature in the app or website.

We accept all major credit cards, digital wallets (Google Pay, Apple Pay), and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our payment gateway.
